Your protection
Section 75 — use it
Pay at least £1 of the purchase price on a credit card. Section 75 of the Consumer Credit Act makes your card provider jointly liable for the full purchase price if the car is misrepresented or not delivered. This is not optional advice. It is the single most powerful protection available to a used car buyer and most people do not use it.
Before you go
Check the VIN plate at viewing — driver's side dashboard, compare to V5C. Any mismatch — walk away.
Pay at least £1 on a credit card for Section 75 protection.
Do a proper test drive — streets, a dual carriageway, not just around the block.
Take someone with you.
