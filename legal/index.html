Who we are

Legit or Leg It is a trading name of Maltings Digital LTD, a company registered in England and Wales. We operate the website legitorlegit.co.uk and provide used car due diligence reports to private buyers.

For the purposes of data protection law, we are the data controller of the personal data we collect from you.

We are registered with the Information Commissioner's Office. Our ICO registration number will appear here once confirmed.

Our infrastructure

We operate a serverless infrastructure using Cloudflare Workers. Personal data is processed in memory during your request and is not written to any persistent storage on our part. When your request is complete, the data is gone.

We do not store reports. Reports are generated on demand, displayed in your browser, and not retained by us. It is your responsibility to save or print your report if you wish to keep a copy.

Our lawful basis for processing

We process data under contract performance — you have asked us to run a check and we are processing the minimum data necessary to do that. We do not rely on consent.

For transaction records, our lawful basis is legal obligation — HMRC requires us to retain these for 6 years.

We do not use your data for marketing, profiling, or any purpose beyond delivering the service you paid for.

What data we collect and why

When you use the free check

Vehicle registration number — this is data about a vehicle, not a person. It is sent to the DVSA via our Cloudflare Worker, processed in memory, and discarded. Never stored.

When you purchase a report

Vehicle registration number and listing URL — not personal data. Processed in memory to generate your report, then discarded.

Payment information — handled entirely by Stripe. We never see, store, or handle your card details or email address. Stripe collects your email for their payment receipt. See stripe.com/gb/privacy.

When you contact us

Your name and email address — used only to respond to your enquiry.

How long we keep your data

  • Report data (reg number, listing URL) — never written to storage. Processed in memory and discarded.
  • Reports — not stored. Generated on demand and displayed in your browser only.
  • Email address — we do not collect your email address as part of the report purchase. Stripe holds it for their payment receipt.
  • Contact enquiries — retained until resolved, then deleted.
  • Transaction records — retained by Stripe and in our accounting records for 6 years, as required by HMRC.

Who we share data with

We do not sell data. We do not share it with third parties for marketing purposes.

The following services receive data only to the extent necessary to run your check:

  • DVSA — receives the vehicle registration number to return MOT history data.
  • Companies House — receives seller or dealer names to return company information.
  • Vehicle history data provider — receives the vehicle registration number to return vehicle history data including finance, condition, mileage, and vehicle image history. Provider to be confirmed.
  • Stripe — handles payment processing. See stripe.com/gb/privacy.
  • Cloudflare — our infrastructure provider. See cloudflare.com/privacypolicy.
  • Anthropic — generates your report from the data retrieved. Report content is not used to train AI models.

Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of any personal data we hold. Given that we collect minimal personal data and do not store reports, in most cases we will hold nothing beyond a transaction record in Stripe.

To exercise your rights, contact us at hello@legitorlegit.co.uk. You also have the right to complain to the Information Commissioner's Office at ico.org.uk.

Cookies

We use only essential cookies required to operate the payment process. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.
